Our Features

Features

MOQ

We adhere to your standard policy of MOQ (Minimum Order Quantity) based pricing. We’d be more than happy to create a tiered level of MOQ for your product line.  

MAP   

We always follow the Map Policies, as it's in the best interest of the consumers and business to maintain MAP pricing on online stores.  

FBA

Amazon FBA has its own advantages with respect to the customer - quick delivery & unbeatable customer support experience. We have expertise to handle direct FBA shipping nationally. Product dimension & weight optimization helps to drastically save FBA fees thus improving the bottomline.  

Advertising

Advertising on Amazon is often termed as PPC (Pay Per Click); this helps immensely to boost sales and ranks for your products. Advertising on amazon requires a high degree of expertise to balance budget allocation and conversions. Steady increase in sales from PPC campaigns helps open up avenues with discovery of products organically on amazon platform.
